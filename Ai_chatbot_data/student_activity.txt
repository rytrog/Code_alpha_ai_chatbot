Student Activities
📌 Overview

AITD encourages students to participate in extracurricular and co-curricular activities for their overall personality development. These activities help students enhance leadership skills, creativity, teamwork, communication abilities, and technical knowledge.

The institute offers a variety of activities through different student cells and clubs.

🎨 Cultural Cell

Students interested in arts, culture, and creative activities can participate in:

🎵 Music Club
💃 Dancing Club
🎭 Nukkad Natak (Street Play) Club
🎬 Stage Play Club
🎨 Decoration Team
📸 Photography Club

🏆 Sports Cell

The Sports Cell promotes physical fitness and sportsmanship through various indoor and outdoor games.

🏏 Cricket
🏸 Badminton
🏐 Volleyball
🎾 Tennis
♟️ Chess
🎯 Carrom
🤼 Kabaddi

✍️ Literary Cell

🎤 Anchoring

🌍 Social & Entrepreneurship Activities
🤝 Social Club

Promotes social awareness and community engagement activities.

Supports innovation, startups, entrepreneurship, and business-related activities.
