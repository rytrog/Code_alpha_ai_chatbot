✅ Eligibility for First Year Admission

• Candidates must have passed or be appearing in the 10+2 (Intermediate) examination.
• Admission is based on JEE Main scores.
• Reservations are applicable as per Government of Uttar Pradesh rules.

🔄 Lateral Entry (Direct Admission to Second Year)

Candidates are eligible for admission to the second year of B.Tech. if they have:

✅ Passed a Diploma in Engineering/Technology from a recognized institution.

OR

✅ Passed B.Sc. with Mathematics from a recognized university.

Admission is based on the CUET examination and applicable admission guidelines.
