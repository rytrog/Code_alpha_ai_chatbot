✅ Eligibility Criteria
Architecture Assistantship

• Must have passed Class 10 (High School).

Computer Science & Engineering

• Must have passed Class 10 (High School).

Modern Office Management & Secretarial Practice (MOM&SP)

• Must have passed Class 12 (Intermediate).
• Hindi and English must be compulsory subjects.

📝 Admission Process

• Admission is based on the Joint Entrance Examination (JEECUP).
• Candidates must have a permanent physical disability of 40% or above.
