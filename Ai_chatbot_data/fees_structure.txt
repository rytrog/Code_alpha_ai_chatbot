AITD Admission Fee Structure
📌 Fresh Admission: First Year & Lateral Entry (Second Year)
👨‍🎓 Non-Handicapped Students

✅ Tuition Fee: ₹60,000
✅ Security Deposit (Refundable): ₹5,000
✅ User Charges: ₹11,000

💰 Total Fee: ₹76,000

♿ Handicapped (Divyangjan) Students

✅ Tuition Fee: ₹45,000
✅ Security Deposit (Refundable): ₹5,000
✅ User Charges: ₹11,000

💰 Total Fee: ₹61,000

🔔 Important Notes

• Security Deposit is refundable.
• Tuition Fee may be revised by the institute in future.
• User Charges include institutional facilities and services.

🎓 Fresh Admission: Second Year, Third Year & Fourth Year
👨‍🎓 Non-Handicapped Students

✅ Tuition Fee: ₹60,000
✅ User Charges: ₹10,500

💰 Total Fee: ₹70,500

♿ Handicapped (Divyangjan) Students

✅ Tuition Fee: ₹45,000
✅ User Charges: ₹10,500

💰 Total Fee: ₹55,500

📝 University Examination Fee

⚠️ Examination Fee is NOT included in the admission fee.

✅ AKTU Examination Fee: ₹7,600

Students must submit this fee separately to AKTU Lucknow during examination form submission.

💳 Available Payment Methods

Students can pay fees through:

✅ UPI
✅ Debit Card
✅ Credit Card
✅ Net Banking
✅ Demand Draft (DD)

🏦 Demand Draft Details

Demand Draft should be made in favor of:

"Director, Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan, U.P., Kanpur"

📍 Payable At: Kanpur
