Training & Placement (T&P) Cell
📌 About the Training & Placement Cell

The Training & Placement (T&P) Cell helps students prepare for internships, training programs, and placement opportunities.

Key Functions

✅ Campus Placement Coordination
✅ Industry Interaction
✅ Career Guidance & Counseling
✅ Internship Support
✅ Placement Training Programs
✅ Recruiter Communication
✅ Student Placement Assistance

The T&P Cell works closely with students, faculty members, and recruiters to provide career opportunities and placement support.
