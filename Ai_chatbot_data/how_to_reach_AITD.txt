Question: How can I reach Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD) from Kanpur Central Railway Station?

Answer:
You can reach Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Awadhpuri, Opposite Rama Dental College, Near Gurudev Chauraha, Kanpur using the following options:

1. Metro + Auto (Recommended)
- Take the metro from Kanpur Central Metro Station.
- Travel towards the Kalyanpur/IIT Kanpur route.
- Get off at Gurudev Chauraha Metro Station.
- Hire an auto-rickshaw or e-rickshaw to reach AITD.
- Estimated travel time: 40–60 minutes.

2. Direct Auto-Rickshaw
- Exit Kanpur Central Railway Station from Platform No. 9 side.
- Hire a direct auto-rickshaw.
- Ask for "AITD Awadhpuri" or "Opposite Rama Dental College".
- Estimated travel time: 35–50 minutes.

3. Cab (Ola/Uber/Taxi)
- Book a cab directly from Kanpur Central Railway Station.
- Estimated travel time: 30–45 minutes.

Destination:
Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Awadhpuri, Opposite Rama Dental College, Near Gurudev Chauraha, Kanpur.
