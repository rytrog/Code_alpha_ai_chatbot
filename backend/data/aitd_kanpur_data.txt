Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

General Information
Full Name: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur
Formerly Known As: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Handicapped (AITH)
Established: 1997 by the Government of Uttar Pradesh under World Bank-assisted Technical Education Project
Type: Government Autonomous Technical Institution
Approved by: AICTE, UGC (2f), and State Government of Uttar Pradesh
Location: Awadhpuri, Opposite Rama Dental College, Kanpur - 208024, Uttar Pradesh, India
Website: www.aitd.ac.in
Campus Area: ~15 acres, fully barrier-free and accessible for Divyang students

Academic Programs Offered

1. Undergraduate (B.Tech - 4 Years)
- Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) - 60 seats
- Information Technology (IT) - 60 seats
- Electronics Engineering (EE) - 60 seats
- Biotechnology (BT) - 60 seats
- Chemical Engineering (CHE) - 60 seats
Total ~300 seats in B.Tech programs

2. Diploma (Polytechnic - 3 Years) (Exclusively for Divyang Students)
- Computer Science & Engineering
- Electronics Engineering
- Architecture Assistantship
Each branch ~40 seats

3. Postgraduate (M.Tech - 2 Years)
Offered in selected branches such as Computer Science and Biotechnology, subject to intake each session.

4. Research and Innovation Programs
Conducted through the Atal Bihari Vajpayee Research Centre (ABVRC) established under DDUQIP, focusing on assistive technology and sustainable innovations for Divyangjan.

Infrastructure and Facilities
- Barrier-free campus with ramps, lifts, wide corridors, tactile paths, and accessible hostels.
- Library: Over 17,800 books, 50+ journals, digital library with NPTEL access.
- Computer Center: 200+ systems, 24x7 internet, LAN and Wi-Fi connectivity.
- Labs and Workshops:
  - CSE/IT: Programming, Data Science, IoT, and AI Labs
  - Electronics: Microprocessor and Embedded Systems Labs
  - Biotechnology: Bioprocess and Genetic Engineering Labs
  - Chemical Engineering: Process Control and Analytical Chemistry Labs
- Hostels: Separate hostels for boys and girls with barrier-free access.
- Sports and Recreation: Indoor/outdoor sports facilities, gymnasium, yoga center.
- Medical and Counselling Unit: On-campus first aid and professional counselling available.

Fee Structure (Approx per year):
- B.Tech: Rs. 65,000 - Rs. 76,000
- Diploma: Rs. 15,000 - Rs. 20,000
- Hostel Fee: Rs. 20,000 - Rs. 25,000
- Special Concessions: Reduced tuition for Divyang students (~Rs. 45,000)

Admissions
- Application Deadline: 30th September (every academic year)
- How to Apply: Online via www.aitd.ac.in/admissions
- Entrance Exams:
  - B.Tech: Based on JEE (Main)
  - Diploma: State-level counselling for Divyang students
- Reservation Policy: 60% seats reserved for differently-abled (Divyang) candidates.

Campus Life and Events
- Major Fests: Shankhnaad, Technozion, Freshers Party, Cultural Week, Sports Fest
- Student Cells and Clubs:
  - Cultural Cell
  - Literary Cell
  - Technical Society
  - NSS Unit
  - Sports and Fitness Club
- Workshops and Seminars: AI bootcamps, Robotics workshops, Bioinnovation summits, Startup mentoring sessions.

Placements
- Placement Cell: Dedicated Training and Placement Department with soft skills training and mock interviews.
- Top Recruiters: TCS, Wipro, Tech Mahindra, Infosys, HCL, and local biotech industries.
- Average Package: Rs. 3 - Rs. 5 LPA (varies by branch)
- Internships: Opportunities in public and private sectors, including CSR projects and R&D labs.

Research and Innovation
ABVRC - Atal Bihari Vajpayee Research Centre:
- Focus on developing assistive technologies for Divyang students.
- Current projects:
  - Low-cost prosthetic devices
  - Water purification and wastewater recycling
  - Eco-friendly materials for packaging
  - Smart mobility devices
