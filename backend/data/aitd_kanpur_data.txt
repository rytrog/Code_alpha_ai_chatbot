# Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

## Institute Overview

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur is a Government of Uttar Pradesh technical institution established in 1997. The institute is dedicated to providing quality technical education to Divyangjan (Persons with Disabilities). It is recognized under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956.

The institute is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) for degree programs and the Board of Technical Education (BTE) for diploma programs.

AITD provides barrier-free infrastructure, accessible facilities, technical education, hostel facilities, placement support, scholarships, and skill development opportunities for students.

The vision of the institute is to create an environment that facilitates quality education, innovation, entrepreneurship, research, skill development, and global opportunities for Divyangjan students.

## Reservation Policy

All diploma programs at AITD have 100% reservation for Divyangjan candidates.

Degree programs have 60% reservation for Divyangjan candidates.

## Degree Programs (B.Tech)

The institute offers the following Bachelor of Technology programs:

Biotechnology – 60 Seats

Chemical Engineering – 60 Seats

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) – 120 Seats

Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) – 60 Seats

Electronics Engineering – 60 Seats

Information Technology – 60 Seats

Admission to the first year of B.Tech programs is based on JEE Main scores. Candidates must have passed or be appearing in the 10+2 examination.

Lateral Entry admission to the second year of B.Tech is available for candidates who have completed a Diploma in Engineering/Technology or a Bachelor of Science degree with Mathematics. Admission is based on CUET and applicable admission guidelines.

## Diploma Programs

The institute offers the following diploma programs:

Architecture Assistantship (3 Years) – 40 Seats

Computer Science and Engineering (3 Years) – 40 Seats

Modern Office Management and Secretarial Practice (2 Years) – 40 Seats

For Architecture Assistantship and Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering, candidates must have passed Class 10.

For Modern Office Management and Secretarial Practice, candidates must have passed Class 12 with Hindi and English as compulsory subjects.

Admission to diploma programs is based on JEECUP. Candidates must possess a permanent disability of 40% or more.

## Campus Location

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan is located at:

Awadhpuri, Opposite Rama Dental College, Near Gurudev Chauraha, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh.

Important landmarks near the institute include Rama Dental College and Gurudev Chauraha.

Students can reach the institute from Kanpur Central Railway Station through Metro and Auto services, direct auto-rickshaws, or app-based taxi services.

Students arriving at Jhakarkati Bus Terminal can reach the institute by direct auto-rickshaw or taxi.

Students arriving at Kanpur Airport can use taxi or auto-rickshaw services to reach the campus.

Travel time from major transportation hubs typically ranges between 30 and 60 minutes depending on traffic conditions.
