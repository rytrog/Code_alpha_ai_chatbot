Question: What is Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD)?

Answer:

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur is a Government of Uttar Pradesh institute established in 1997 for providing quality technical education to Divyangjan (Persons with Disabilities).

The institute is recognized under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956 and is affiliated with:

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) for Degree Programs
Board of Technical Education (BTE) for Diploma Programs

AITD offers Degree and Diploma programs with accessible and barrier-free infrastructure, hostel facilities, placement support, scholarship opportunities, and a supportive learning environment for Divyangjan students.

Reservation Policy:

Diploma Programs: 100% seats are reserved for Divyangjan candidates.
Degree Programs: 60% seats are reserved for Divyangjan candidates and 40% seats are available for non-Divyangjan candidates as per applicable admission rules.

Vision:
To create an environment that promotes quality education, research, innovation, entrepreneurship, skill development, and global opportunities for Divyangjan students.

Question: What is the admission fee structure at AITD for First Year and Lateral Entry students?

Answer:

For Non-Handicapped Students

Tuition Fee: ₹60,000
Security Deposit (Refundable): ₹5,000
User Charges: ₹11,000
Total Fee: ₹76,000

For Handicapped (Divyangjan) Students

Tuition Fee: ₹45,000
Security Deposit (Refundable): ₹5,000
User Charges: ₹11,000
Total Fee: ₹61,000

Note: Lateral Entry (Direct Second Year Admission) follows the same fee structure as First Year admission.

Question: Is the AKTU examination fee included in the admission fee at AITD?

Answer:

No. The AKTU examination fee is not included in the admission fee.

AKTU Examination Fee: ₹7,600
This fee must be paid separately to AKTU Lucknow during examination form submission.
