Department Overview

The Department of Electronics Engineering at Dr. Ambedkar Institute of
Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur was established in 2004. The
department is specially designed for Divyang students and provides
quality technical education through modern laboratories, smart
classrooms, experienced faculty members, research activities,
innovation, and outcome-based education.

Department Name: Electronics Engineering

Academic Wing: Degree Wing

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Kanpur

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. When was the department established? A. 2004.

Q. What program is offered? A. B.Tech. in Electronics Engineering with
an intake of 60 students.

Q. Does the department have a Centre of Excellence? A. Yes, in
collaboration with Texas Instruments.

Q. What laboratories are available? A. Electronics Circuits, PCB Design,
Digital & Logic Design, Network Engineering, Electronic Measurement,
Communication Systems, Control Systems, Fibre Optics, Microwave &
Antenna Design, and CAD Laboratory.

Q. Does the department have IEEE activities? A. Yes. The department has
an IEEE subsection that organizes technical programs and competitions.
