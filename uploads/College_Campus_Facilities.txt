# Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

# Campus Facilities

## Campus Overview

### About the Campus

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur is a Government Technical Institute located in Awadhpuri, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh.

The institute provides an inclusive, accessible, and barrier-free campus designed for Divyangjan (Persons with Disabilities) as well as general students. The campus supports quality technical education, practical learning, research, innovation, student welfare, and overall personality development.

---

### Campus Information

Institute Name: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

Location: Awadhpuri, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh

Campus Area: 15 Acres

Campus Type: Accessible Barrier-Free Campus

Academic Environment: Inclusive Technical Education Campus

Road Connectivity: Approximately 500 Meters from Grand Trunk Road (GT Road / National Highway)
