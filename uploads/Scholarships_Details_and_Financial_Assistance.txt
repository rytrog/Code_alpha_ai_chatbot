## Scholarship Application Process

### Step 1
Identify the applicable scholarship scheme.

### Step 2
Read eligibility conditions.

### Step 3
Collect all required documents.

### Step 4
Register on the respective scholarship portal.

### Step 5
Complete the online application.

### Step 6
Upload supporting documents.

### Step 7
Submit the application before the deadline.

### Step 8
Complete institute verification if required.

### Step 9
Track application status.

### Step 10
Receive scholarship through DBT after approval.
