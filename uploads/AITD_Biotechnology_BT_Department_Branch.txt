Department of Biotechnology

Department Overview

The Department of Biotechnology at Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology
for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur was established in 2008. The department
provides quality biotechnology education, research exposure, innovation,
practical laboratory training, and industry-oriented learning. The
curriculum is designed to prepare students for careers in biotechnology,
pharmaceuticals, agriculture, healthcare, environmental science,
industrial biotechnology, research, and higher education.
