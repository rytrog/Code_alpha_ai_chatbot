Team Overview

The Training & Placement (T&P) Cell works with students, faculty,
departments, and industry partners to coordinate placements,
internships, industrial training, skill development, and career
guidance.

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Kanpur
