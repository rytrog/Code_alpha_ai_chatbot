Service Overview

Student Counseling Services at Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for
Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur provides academic, personal, emotional,
social, and career guidance to support student well-being, confidence,
and overall development.

Service Name: Student Counseling Services

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Kanpur

Service Category: Student Support Services

-   Provide academic guidance.
