# AITD Campus Navigation Guide

## Campus Overview
Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur has an inclusive, barrier-free 15-acre campus designed for independent mobility and equal access for all students, including Divyangjan (Persons with Disabilities).

## Campus Entry Points

### Main Gate
#### Location
Located directly on Awadhpuri to barsaitpur Road in oppsition of Rama Dental College ,Khyora, Kanpur.
#### Nearby Landmark
Located opposite Rama Dental College.
#### Directions
Main entry point for all vehicles, students,staff, guests, and daily commuters. Follow the main driveway straight to reach the security checkpoint which is Second Gate.

### Second Gate
#### Location
Located approximately 100 meters inside the campus from the Main Gate,which is Security Gate.
#### Connected Roads
Connected directly to the Main Gate driveway and the inner campus pathways,in middle of this road there is mess for college students.
#### Available Paths
from Second gate it splits into two primary routes: the Right Path (leading to Main Building,Girls Hostel,Playground and the Faculty residence) and the Left Path (leading to the backyard Canteen,New H-block,, and Biotechnology departments,Chemical departments,Polytechnic Wing).

---

# Campus Navigation

## Route from Main Gate to Second Gate
Walk straight from the Main Gate along the wide filled with trees,flowers plants,greenery,paved driveway for approximately 100 meters to reach the Second Gate.
### Walking Distance
Approximately 100 meters.
### Estimated Walking Time
Around 1 to 2 minutes at a regular pace.
### Landmarks
Pass the administrative boundary and the outer campus gardens on your right.

---

## Route from Second Gate to Main Academic Building
From the Second Gate, you can choose Right turn path main routes to reach the Main Academic Building.

### Right Path
#### Distance
Approximately 50 meters.
#### Buildings Passed
Passes H Block and B Block on your left.
#### Destination
Leads directly to the front main entrance of the Main Academic Building, there is a bigger playground when you go straight ahead.

### Left Path
#### Distance
Approximately 50 meters.
#### Buildings Passed
Passes the H-Block on your Right side and after going 50 meters you will Biotechnology Department and Chemical Engineering Department building on your right side.
#### Destination
Leads to the backyard canteen,New H-Block, Biotechnology departments,Chemical departments,Polytechnic Wing..

# Main Academic Building

## Ground Floor
The main hub for student services, administration,Scholarship Cell, Academic Office, Academic Directorate, Computer Laboratory,Director Office and support offices.

### Reception Hall
#### Waiting Area
A spacious, comfortable seating hall directly inside the main entrance for visitors and students.
#### Student Help Area
Located near the reception desk to assist with admissions, schedules, and general queries.
#### Lift
A lift in front of both hallway connected with left and right side of Reception Area and it is equipped with an accessible elevator providing easy travel to the upper floors.
#### Staircase
Located when you go straight to left hallway then take left turn you will find the stairs and when you go straight to right hallway then take right turn you reach in Workshop and laboratories all branches.

---

## Academic Section

### Academic Office
Located on the Ground Floor of the Main Academic Building. walk to the right corridor and take first left turn; the office is on the left corridor, or you can make direct queris from waiting area.

### Computer Laboratory
The primary ground floor programming lab is situated in the first left room,in Rigth hallway.

### Director Office
Located on the Ground Floor of the Main Academic Building, next to the Computer Laboratory and director office for official interactions.

### Scholarship Section
Located on the Ground Floor of the Main Academic Building,in right hallway from main entrance process fee waivers and reimbursements.

## First Floor
Houses main academic learning facilities and quiet study environments.

### Library
The Central Library is located on the First Floor,In front of Seminar Hall, containing textbook sections, reference books, and academic journals.

### Reading Hall
A spacious, quiet study area situated inside the Central Library for students and faculty.

### Computer Laboratory
Additional computer labs for advanced programming and online tests are located on the First Floor when you further go to Right hallway after passing library from lift.

### Lift Access
Fully accessible lift lobby situated on the First Floor corridor connect with Seminar Hall and with  all floors.

# Common Navigation Queries

## From Main Gate

### To Director Office
Enter the Main Gate, walk 100 meters to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, enter the Main Academic Building, and the Director Office is on the Ground Floor near the Academic Office.

### To Library
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take Right Path to the Main Academic Building, and take the lift or stairs to the First Floor.

### To Scholarship Cell
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, enter the Main Academic Building, and go to the Ground Floor in left hallway from waiting area.

### To Academic Office
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, enter the Main Academic Building, and locate the office on the Ground Floor corridor on the Right side.

### To Playground
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, pass the Main building towards the left. The playground is on your left.

### To Girls Hostel
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, pass the Main building towards the left and walk straight for 150 meters. Girls Hostel is one more turn away to left.

### To Biotechnology Department
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Left Path, and follow the path behind the Main Academic Building to E Block.

### To Polytechnic Wing
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Left Path, pass E Block, and continue straight to the Polytechnic Wing.

### To H Block
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Left Path. H Block is the first building on your right.

### To B Block
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, enter in main academic building and go to left hallway the first left turn is B Block.

### To F Block
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, enter in main academic building and go to left hallway or take lift to reach in f block.

### To Computer Laboratory
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Right Path, enter the Main Academic Building. Labs are on the Ground and First Floors.

### To Mess
Enter the Main Gate, walk to the Second Gate, take the Left Path, and walk past the h block to the hostel mess hall.

# Campus Facilities

## Drinking Water
Purified, cold drinking water units are installed on every floor of the Main Academic Building and near all academic blocks.

## Washrooms
Separate, clean, and wheelchair-accessible washrooms are available on all floors of the Main Academic Building and inside all blocks.

## Lift
An elevator is available inside the Main Academic Building for Divyangjan accessibility and student convenience.

## Accessibility Facilities
Ramps, tactile paths, wheelchair-friendly toilets, and lifts are provided across all academic and residential buildings.

## Parking
Dedicated parking zones for student and staff vehicles are located near the Second Gate.

## Waiting Hall
A seating area is available on the Ground Floor of the Main Academic Building next to the reception.

## Security
24/7 security guard monitoring and CCTV surveillance are active across the entire campus, including academic blocks and residential zones.

## Greenery
The campus is known for its lush greenery and beautiful natural surroundings. There are many trees, gardens, and parks throughout the campus, making it a pleasant place to walk around and enjoy nature. The greenery also contributes to the peaceful and serene atmosphere of the campus.
