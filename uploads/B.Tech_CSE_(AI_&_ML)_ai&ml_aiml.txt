# Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

## B.Tech Programs

### Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

#### Program Overview

Program Name: B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning)

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

Degree: Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

Specialization: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

Affiliation: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU)

Approval: AICTE (All India Council for Technical Education)

Intake Capacity: 60 Seats

---

#### Program Description

The B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) programme at AITD Kanpur offers a comprehensive undergraduate curriculum designed to equip students with advanced knowledge in Computer Science along with specialized Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies.

## Important Programme Note

### Specialization Information

The Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML) branch is a specialization under the Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) programme.

It is not a separate academic department.

The specialization focuses primarily on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies while retaining the core Computer Science curriculum.
