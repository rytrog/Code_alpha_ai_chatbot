
# Scholarship Documents Checklist

## Overview

This document is a structured RAG-ready checklist of documents commonly required for scholarship applications. Requirements vary depending on the scholarship scheme, institute, and government department. Verify all requirements with the latest official notification before submission.

# Core Documents

## Identity Documents

### Aadhaar Card
- Aadhaar card of the student
- Aadhaar should be linked with the registered mobile number
- Recommended to be linked with the bank account for DBT

### Passport Size Photographs
- Recent passport-size colour photographs
- As per scholarship portal specifications

### Bank Passbook
- Student's bank passbook
- Account holder name
- Account number
- IFSC code
- Active account for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)

# Financial Documents

## Income Certificate

### Requirements
- Issued by the competent authority
- Valid for the applicable academic session
- Income limit depends on the scholarship scheme

## Family Details
- Parent/Guardian details
- Occupation details (if required)

### Purpose
Proof of permanent residence where required.

- Address proof if requested

# Disability Documents

## Disability Certificate

### Requirements
- Issued by an authorized medical board
- Percentage of disability mentioned
- Valid certificate

## UDID Card

### Purpose
Unique Disability Identity Card for eligible students.

# Bank and DBT Documents

## Bank Account
- Student-owned account preferred
- Active account

## Aadhaar Seeding
- Aadhaar linked to bank account

- Recommended for successful DBT transfer

# Best Practices

## Before Applying
- Read official guidelines
- Check eligibility
- Prepare scanned copies
- Verify document validity

## Before Final Submission
- Review uploaded documents
- Verify bank details
- Save acknowledgement receipt
- Track application status regularly
