# Reaching Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur

## Category
Transportation | Location | Travel Guide | Routes | Path

## Overview

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur is located on Awadhpuri Road, Khyora, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. The campus is well connected by road, rail, and public transportation. Students, parents, faculty members, visitors, and guests can conveniently reach the institute from Kanpur Central Railway Station, Lucknow, Jhakarkati Bus Stand, Rawatpur, and other parts of Kanpur using trains, buses, auto-rickshaws, e-rickshaws, taxis, app-based cab services, or private vehicles.

---

# Route from Kanpur Central Railway Station to AITD

## Overview

Kanpur Central Railway Station (CNB) is the nearest major railway station to Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur.

### Distance

Approximately **11.5 kilometres**

### Estimated Travel Time

Approximately **35–45 minutes**, depending on traffic conditions.

## Step-by-Step Route

### Step 1 – Exit the Railway Station

Exit Kanpur Central Railway Station from **Platform Number 1 (City Side)**. This side contains:

- Main parking area
- Auto-rickshaw stand
- E-rickshaw stand
- Taxi stand
- Pickup point for app-based cabs

### Step 2 – Reach the Main Highway

Take **Jaipuria Road (Railway Station Road)** and merge onto **NH-34 (Grand Trunk Road / Kanpur–Allahabad Highway).**

### Step 3 – Continue Along NH-34

Drive towards **Rawatpur** and **Kalyanpur**.

During the journey you will pass important intersections including:

- GTI Crossing
- Gurudev Palace Chauraha
- Other major city junctions

### Step 4 – Turn Towards Awadhpuri Road

Near the **HBTI West Campus** boundary, take the turn towards **Awadhpuri Road**.

### Step 5 – Continue Straight

Continue driving along Awadhpuri Road.

### Step 6 – Pass the Landmark

You will pass **Rama Dental College**, an important landmark near the institute.

### Step 7 – Reach AITD

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur is located on the **left side of Awadhpuri Road**.

# Transportation Options from Kanpur Central Railway Station

## Reserve Auto-Rickshaw

### Availability

Available directly outside Platform Number 1.

### Fare

₹150–₹250

### Recommended For

- Students with luggage
- Parents
- Faculty
- Visitors
- First-time travellers

---

## Shared Auto-Rickshaw

### Route

Take a shared auto to:

- Gurudev Crossing
- Rawatpur Chauraha

### Fare

₹20–₹30

### Last-Mile Travel

From Rawatpur or Gurudev Crossing, take an e-rickshaw directly to AITD.

### E-Rickshaw Fare

₹10–₹20

# Route from Lucknow to AITD Kanpur

## Overview

Students travelling from Lucknow can reach AITD by train, bus, private car, taxi, or other road transport.

### Distance

Approximately **98–105 kilometres**

### Estimated Travel Time

Approximately **2.5–3 hours**

---

## Road Route

### Step 1 – Leave Lucknow

Start via:

- Shaheed Path
- VIP Road

Merge onto **NH-27 (Lucknow–Kanpur Highway).**

### Step 2 – Travel on NH-27

Continue via:

- Amausi
- Unnao

NH-27 is a multi-lane national highway with toll plazas.

### Step 3 – Enter Kanpur

Cross the **Jajmau Ganga Bridge**.

### Step 4 – Follow NH-34

Cross the Jajmau Flyover and keep left following signs for:

- GT Road
- NH-34
- Rawatpur
- Kalyanpur

### Step 5 – Reach Awadhpuri Road

Turn onto **Awadhpuri Road** near **Khyora**.

### Step 6 – Reach AITD

Continue until the main entrance of Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD).

# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## Which is the nearest railway station to AITD?

Kanpur Central Railway Station is the nearest major railway station.

## How far is AITD from Kanpur Central Railway Station?

Approximately **11.5 kilometres**.

## How much time does it take?

Around **35–45 minutes**.

## Is a direct auto available?

Yes. Reserve auto-rickshaws are available outside Platform Number 1.

## Are Ola, Uber, and Rapido available?

Yes. All major ride services operate in Kanpur.

## What is the cheapest travel option?

Shared auto (₹20–₹30) followed by an e-rickshaw (₹10–₹20).

## What is the best way to travel from Lucknow?

Travelling by train from Lucknow Charbagh Railway Station to Kanpur Central Railway Station is generally the most economical and convenient option.

## Which bus station is nearest?

Jhakarkati Bus Stand.

## Can I reach AITD by private car?

Yes. The campus is well connected through NH-27, NH-34, and Awadhpuri Road.
