Institute Overview

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur is a
Government of Uttar Pradesh technical institute established to provide
quality technical education for Divyangjan (Persons with Disabilities).

Institute Name: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan
(AITD), Kanpur

Established: 1997

Institute Type: Government Technical Institute

Recognition: Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956

Primary Focus: Technical Education for Divyangjan Students

admission rules.
