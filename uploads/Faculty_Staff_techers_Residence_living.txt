Faculty & Staff Residence

Residence Overview

The Faculty and Staff Residence at Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology
for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur provides on-campus residential
accommodation for faculty members and institute staff. The residential
campus offers a safe, secure, and convenient living environment with
easy access to academic and administrative facilities.

Facility Name: Faculty & Staff Residence

Location: AITD Campus

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Kanpur

Facility Type: Residential Quarters
