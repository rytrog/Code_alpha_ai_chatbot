Professional Introduction

Prof. (Dr.) Rachna Asthana is an academic leader, researcher,
administrator, and educator with more than three decades of experience
in higher education. Her expertise spans teaching, research,
institutional development, academic administration, curriculum
development, quality assurance, and technical education governance.

Since assuming the role of Director of AITD in March 2018, she has led
several institutional development initiatives focused on academic
excellence, accessibility, research, infrastructure modernization,
industry collaboration, and student development.
