Department Overview

The Department of Information Technology (IT) at Dr. Ambedkar Institute
of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD), Kanpur was established in 2007. The
department is committed to quality technical education, research,
innovation, industry-oriented learning, leadership development, and
ethical professional practice.

Department Name: Information Technology (IT)

Academic Wing: Degree Wing

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Kanpur

Established: 2007

Program Offered: Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) in Information
Technology

Approved Intake: 60 Students

Vision

Create a highly conducive environment for quality academics, research,
and industry-oriented education in Information Technology while
producing competent professionals with research, innovation, leadership,
and ethical values for the betterment of Divyang students, non-Divyang
students, and society.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mission

M1: Provide quality education and academic excellence.

M2: Develop leadership, accountability, and professionalism.

M3: Continuously upgrade faculty and supporting staff.

M4: Strengthen laboratories and practical learning.

M5: Produce highly skilled graduates through strong industry
interaction.
