Placement Cell Overview

The Training & Placement Cell serves as the interface between the
institute and industry. It coordinates campus recruitment, internships,
industrial training, skill development, and industry collaboration to
enhance student employability.

Institute: Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology for Divyangjan (AITD),
Kanpur
