Option 1: Header-based CSV (Recommended)
This uses explicit headers so the system knows exactly which columns correspond to the questions and answers, even if they are in different columns or order.

csv
Question,Answer
"What are the college timings?","AITD Kanpur office timings are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Classes generally run from 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM."
"Is there a canteen on campus?","Yes, there is a central canteen providing hygienic snacks, meals, and beverages for students and staff at subsidized rates."
"Who is the Head of Electronics Department?","Dr. Manoj Kumar is the Head of the Electronics Engineering Department at AITD Kanpur."
"What is the contact email for support?","For general support, you can email helpdesk@aitd.ac.in. For admission queries, email admissions@aitd.ac.in."
Option 2: Plain No-Header (Index-based) CSV
If you do not want headers, you can upload a CSV where the first column is the question and the second column is the answer.




csv
"Where is the seminar hall?","The central seminar hall is located on the second floor of the main administrative block."
"What is the intake for B.Tech CS?","The B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering department has an annual intake of approximately 60 students."
"Are there sports facilities?","Yes, AITD has facilities for indoor and outdoor sports including basketball, volleyball, badminton, table tennis, and a campus playground."









Instructions to Upload:
Copy either of the code blocks above.
Paste it into a text file and save it with a .csv extension (e.g. aitd_faq_test.csv).
Go to the Admin Panel: Documents Page (http://localhost:8000/admin/upload.html).
Select Q&A / FAQ Dataset (.csv) as the Document Type.
Drag and drop or browse to select your saved .csv file.
Click Upload & Ingest.
Test the questions instantly on the chatbot page!
